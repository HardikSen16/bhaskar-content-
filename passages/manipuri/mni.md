With over 1.8 million native speakers, Manipuri, also known as Meitei, is the official language of the state of Manipur in northeastern India. It is the only Sino-Tibetan language included among the 22 scheduled languages of the Indian Constitution. Manipuri is traditionally written in the Meitei Mayek script, an ancient script that has been revived and reintroduced in recent decades after centuries of dominance by the Bengali script.
